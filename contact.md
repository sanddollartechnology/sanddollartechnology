## Frequently Asked Questions

**How quickly can you start?**  
Typically within 1-2 weeks, depending on our current commitments and the scope of your project. For urgent needs, we can often accommodate faster starts.

**What's your pricing model?**  
We offer flexible pricing based on the engagement model—fixed price for projects, monthly retainers for ongoing work, and hourly rates for staff augmentation. We'll provide transparent pricing after understanding your needs.

**Do you work with startups?**  
Absolutely! We love working with startups and understand the unique constraints and pace required. We offer flexible arrangements to accommodate early-stage budgets.

**Can you help with projects already in progress?**  
Yes! Whether you need help unblocking a stuck project, accelerating delivery, or providing specialized expertise, we can jump in at any stage.

**What industries do you serve?**  
We work across industries—from healthcare and finance to retail and manufacturing. Our focus is on the technology, not the industry, which allows us to bring fresh perspectives.
